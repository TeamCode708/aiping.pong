AI Ping Pong







Instructions

1. Keep your device screen straight.
2. Keep 3-4 feet distance from the device.
3. Before pressing the play button, hold out your right wrist and check if the red dot is being placed on the wrist, if not then refresh the page once. (NOTE: It might take a few seconds for the red dot to appear)
4. In order to play the game, move your right wrist up & down to move the paddle. (NOTE: The red paddle is you).
5. Try avoiding white background.
